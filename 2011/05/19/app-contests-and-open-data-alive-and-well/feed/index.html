]]> By: Luigi Montanez https://civic.io/2011/05/19/app-contests-and-open-data-alive-and-well/#comment-158 Thu, 19 May 2011 20:44:34 +0000 http://www.voiceingov.org/blog/?p=2307#comment-158 One thing I’d like to see is more followthrough from governments *after* the apps contests have ended.

At Sunlight, the biggest success we point to from the contest model came out of our second Apps for America contest, where an entry (GovPulse.us) became an actual federal website in FederalRegister.gov. In that case, some forward-thinking folks at the Office of the Federal Register contacted the development team behind GovPulse and contracted them to do a .gov version. FederalRegister.gov is now an open source app available on GitHub.

Governments all around the world are promoting innovation through these contests and the release of open data. But they need to embrace that innovation by contracting out the best projects for further, serious development.
